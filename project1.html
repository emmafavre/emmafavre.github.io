Insurance Fraud Detection System

SUMMARY

I developed this project during the first year of my Master’s degree. As my very first foray into the field of data analytics, it represents my transition from foundational theory to real-world application. The goal was to assist an insurance provider in identifying high-risk fraudulent claims. By analyzing a dataset of 1,100 declarations, I applied statistical testing and machine learning algorithms to build a system capable of flagging suspicious behavior, helping the company optimize its manual review process. The full report is written in French.
